About Kavian Development Mine

Leading the Future of Mining Engineering with Innovation and Excellence

Who We Are

Founded in 2005, Kavian Development Mine has established itself as a premier mining engineering consultancy firm, delivering world-class solutions to clients across 30 countries. With over 200 successfully completed projects and a team of 150+ expert engineers, we combine technical excellence with innovative approaches to solve the most complex mining challenges.

Our expertise spans the entire mining value chain, from initial feasibility studies through plant design, equipment selection, and operational optimization. We are committed to sustainable mining practices and leverage cutting-edge technologies including AI-powered cost estimation and advanced process simulation.
