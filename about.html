Who We Are

Kavian Development Mine is a leading mining engineering consultancy with over 15 years of experience in the industry. We specialize in providing comprehensive solutions for mining projects worldwide, from initial feasibility studies to plant commissioning and optimization. Our team of expert engineers combines deep technical knowledge with practical experience to deliver innovative, cost-effective, and sustainable mining solutions.

Our Journey

2010

Foundation

Kavian Development Mine was established by a team of experienced mining engineers with a vision to revolutionize mining consultancy services in the region.

2013

First Major Project

Successfully completed our first large-scale copper processing plant design, establishing our reputation for excellence in mineral processing engineering.

International Expansion

Expanded operations to serve clients across Middle East, Asia, and Africa, bringing world-class mining engineering expertise to emerging markets.

Industry Leadership

Recognized as one of the top mining consultancy firms in the region, with over 200 successful projects and numerous industry awards.
