Comprehensive Feasibility Studies

Professional feasibility studies compliant with international standards (NI 43-101, JORC, SAMREC) providing investors with reliable technical and economic evaluations for informed decision-making.

Overview

Feasibility studies are critical documents that determine whether a mining project should proceed to development. Our feasibility study services provide comprehensive technical, economic, and financial evaluations that meet international reporting standards and satisfy the requirements of investors, lenders, and regulatory authorities.

We have completed over 150 feasibility studies for projects ranging from small-scale operations to world-class mining developments. Our multidisciplinary team includes qualified persons (QPs) under NI 43-101 and competent persons under JORC/SAMREC codes.
