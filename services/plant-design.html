ENGINEERING SERVICES

Complete Plant Design & Engineering

Full-spectrum engineering services from conceptual design through detailed engineering, procurement support, and construction management for mining and mineral processing facilities.

Overview

Plant design and engineering is at the core of successful mining project development. Our comprehensive engineering services cover all aspects of plant design from initial concept through detailed engineering and construction support. We deliver integrated, optimized designs that maximize operational efficiency while minimizing capital and operating costs.

With over 200 successfully completed plant designs ranging from 1,000 to 100,000 tonnes per day capacity, our team brings unparalleled expertise in process engineering, mechanical design, electrical systems, instrumentation and control, civil and structural engineering, and project management.
