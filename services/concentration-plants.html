Mineral Concentration Plants

Complete design and engineering services for mineral concentration plants including crushing, grinding, flotation, gravity separation, and magnetic separation for copper, gold, iron ore, and other minerals.

Overview

Mineral concentration is the process of separating valuable minerals from gangue materials to produce a concentrate suitable for further processing. Our concentration plant engineering services cover all aspects from conceptual design through detailed engineering, procurement support, and commissioning assistance.

With over 200 successfully completed concentration plant projects worldwide, we have extensive experience in designing plants ranging from 1,000 to 100,000 tonnes per day. Our team includes specialists in crushing, grinding, flotation, gravity separation, magnetic separation, and dewatering technologies.
