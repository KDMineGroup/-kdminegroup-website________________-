HYDROMETALLURGY

Heap & Tank Leaching Systems

Comprehensive engineering services for heap leaching, tank leaching, and pressure oxidation systems for copper, gold, nickel, and uranium extraction with optimized recovery and environmental compliance.

Overview

Leaching is a hydrometallurgical process that uses aqueous solutions to extract valuable metals from ores. Our leaching system engineering services encompass all aspects of design, from pad construction and solution management to pregnant leach solution (PLS) processing and metal recovery.

We have designed leaching systems for operations ranging from small-scale heap leach pads processing 5,000 tonnes per day to large tank leaching circuits handling 50,000 tonnes per day. Our expertise covers acid leaching, cyanide leaching, ammonia leaching, and pressure oxidation technologies.
