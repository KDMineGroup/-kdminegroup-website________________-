Vendor Partnerships & Procurement Support

Comprehensive procurement services leveraging our global network of qualified vendors to deliver competitive pricing, reliable equipment, and seamless project execution for mining operations worldwide.

Overview

Successful project execution depends on selecting the right equipment suppliers and managing the procurement process effectively. Our vendor partnership and procurement services help clients navigate the complex landscape of mining equipment suppliers, negotiate favorable terms, and ensure timely delivery of quality equipment.

With established relationships with over 200 leading equipment manufacturers and suppliers worldwide, we provide clients with access to competitive pricing, technical support, and proven equipment solutions. Our procurement team has managed equipment packages valued at over $2 billion for mining projects globally.

Procurement Process

1. Requirements Definition

Detailed technical specifications, performance requirements, and commercial terms are defined based on project needs.

2. Vendor Identification

Qualified vendors are identified from our global network and invited to participate in the bidding process.

3. RFQ & Bid Collection

Request for quotations are issued and vendor proposals are collected and organized for evaluation.

4. Technical & Commercial Evaluation

Detailed evaluation of technical compliance, pricing, delivery schedules, and commercial terms.

5. Contract Award & Management

Contract finalization, purchase order issuance, and ongoing vendor management throughout delivery.
